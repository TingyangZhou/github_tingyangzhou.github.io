Small Cats Big Cats
Persian cats are gentle, quiet, docile. They only give their attention to people they trust. Siamese cats are among the most vocal of cats. Their coloring develops after exposure to cold. Lions are the only social cats and live in family groups called prides. Jaguars are the largest cats in the Americas.They are solitary animals and prefer to live alone.
American shorthairs cats are known for their friendly and outgoing personalities. Hairless Sphynx cats are often described as 'extroverts' and enjoy socializing with people and other pets. Tigers are known for their distinctive orange fur with black stripes. They have striped skin, not just striped fur. Leopards are able to climb trees very well, and are known for their ability to carry their prey up a tree.
